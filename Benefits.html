What Is The Benefits Of 5G To Business And Society?

Autonomous Driving

5G's low latency means we could see self-driving cars becoming more common, with roads connected with transmitters and sensors that can send and receive
information to vehicles in 1/1,000 of a second. Less time is critical for AI and radar technology to interpret what they see (other cars, pedestrians,
stop signs) and control the car accordingly.

Smart Factory

5G enables the Internet of Things , which means factories can wirelessly connect thousands of smart devices, such as cameras and sensors, to automatically
collect data in real time. Factories can analyze and process this data to improve operational efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

Environment

5G has the potential to help reduce global emissions. One of the advantages of 5G compared to previous networks is the transmission efficiency and the low
power consumption it uses. It will also enable real-time monitoring of emissions, air quality, water quality and other environmental indicators.
