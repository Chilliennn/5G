History of 5G

5G, short for the fifth generation of wireless technology, builds upon its predecessors (2G, 3G, and 4G) and promises to bring even faster data transfer speeds, lower latency, and more reliable connectivity.The concept of 5G started taking shape around 2010 when researchers and industry leaders began discussing the need for a new standard to meet the ever-increasing demand for data in our connected world.In 2018, the 3rd Generation Partnership Project (3GPP) established the initial standards for 5G, laying the foundation for its development.
