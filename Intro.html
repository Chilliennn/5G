What Is 5G?


5G stands for the fifth generation of wireless technology, offering faster data speeds and lower latency compared to its predecessors.
How does 5G work? It uses higher-frequency radio waves and advanced antenna technology to transmit data more efficiently.
What are the benefits of 5G? It enables faster internet speeds, improved connectivity for IoT devices, and supports emerging technologies like autonomous vehicles and augmented reality.

Is 5G Safe?

Extensive research has shown that 5G technology is safe, with exposure levels well below international guidelines.
