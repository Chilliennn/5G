Development of 5G Globally

5G deployment has been rapidly advancing worldwide. Countries like the United States, China, South Korea, and Japan have been at the forefront of
5G development, with widespread commercial networks. Europe has also made significant progress. Telecom companies and governments across the
globe are investing heavily in 5G infrastructure to stay competitive and support new technologies.

5G In Malaysia

Malaysia, like many other countries, is actively working on implementing 5G technology. The Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission
(MCMC) has initiated trials and collaborations with telecommunications providers to introduce 5G networks. The Malaysian government aims to
make 5G available to the public by 2022, with the potential to drive economic growth and technological innovation in the country.
